Project Overview

This project involved creating an interactive financial dashboard for Microsoft Contoso Ltd to track key financial metrics and provide actionable insights for business decision-making. The dashboard allows stakeholders to monitor revenue streams, analyze expense patterns, and evaluate profitability across different business units.
